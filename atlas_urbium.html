Atlas Urbium Graphics

Interactive web graphics for Atlas Urbium.

2022

This series of interactive webpages was made for the first case study of Atlas Urbium, a crowdfunded research project. The design was inspired by the bold graphic style of screenprinted posters, and the webpages were coded in Javascript/JQuery. Shown here are: a catalog of Shaker villages, an interactive timeline of the Shaker population, a catalog of Shaker inventions, and an animation of Shaker service rituals.
